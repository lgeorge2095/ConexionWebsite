What is an independent insurance agency?

An independent agency like Conexion represents multiple insurance carriers — not just one. That means we shop around on your behalf and compare options from 20+ top-rated companies to find you the best coverage at the best price. We work for you, not the insurance company.

How do I get a quote?

It's easy — just click Get a Quote on our website, choose your coverage type, and fill out the form. You can also call us at (913) 490-9050 and we'll walk you through everything. Most quotes take less than 10 minutes.

  • Homeowners & Renters Insurance
  • Business & Commercial Insurance
  • Workers' Compensation
  • Life Insurance (Term, Whole, Universal)
  • RV, Boat & Roadside Assistance
  • Umbrella Insurance

How much does insurance cost?

The cost depends on many factors — your location, driving history, the type of coverage, and more. Because we work with 20+ carriers, we're often able to find rates that beat what you're currently paying. The best way to find out is to get a free quote — there's no obligation.

Can I bundle my home and auto insurance?

Absolutely. Bundling your home and auto insurance is one of the easiest ways to save money. Many of our carriers offer significant multi-policy discounts. We'll compare bundled vs. separate options to make sure you're getting the best deal either way.

What should I do if I need to file a claim?

You can file a claim directly with your insurance carrier — we'll provide you with their contact information when you get your policy. You're also welcome to call our office at (913) 490-9050 and we'll help guide you through the process.

How often should I review my insurance coverage?

We recommend reviewing your coverage at least once a year, or any time you have a major life change — like buying a home, getting married, having a child, or starting a business. Your needs change, and your coverage should too. We're happy to do a free review anytime.
