Privacy Policy

How Conexion Insurance Agency collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: June 2024

Conexion Insurance Agency respects your privacy and is committed to explaining how we collect and use information through our website at www.conexionins.com and any linked websites or mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Non-Personal Information: When you visit our site, we may automatically collect information such as your browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, access dates and times, internet service provider, and referring website.

Personal Information: We collect personal information — such as your name, address, or other contact information, policy information, or information provided for roadside assistance services — only when you voluntarily provide it. With your express consent, we may also collect mobile device location data.

Children's Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not submit any personal information through our website or services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the products and services you request
  • Process orders and transactions
  • Deliver marketing and promotional communications
  • Improve our services and the usefulness of our website
  • Customize your experience
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Prevent fraud and misuse of our services

Information Sharing

We do not sell or disclose your personal information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • You have given us authorization to share it
  • Contractors or service providers need it to deliver services on our behalf
  • It is necessary to deliver a product or complete a transaction
  • It is shared with marketing partners in connection with our services
  • We are involved in a corporate merger, acquisition, or restructuring
  • We are required to do so by law or legal process

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to recognize your browser, tailor content to your interests, and analyze how our website is used. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, though doing so may limit certain website functionality. We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.

Security

We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. Sensitive information transmitted through our website is protected using SSL encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

Interactive Features

Any content you submit to public areas of our website, such as blogs or forums, carries no expectation of privacy and may be viewed by other users.
